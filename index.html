OPEN At Wits

Locations

OPEN, Maboneng, Johannesburg

In the heart of Maboneng, OPEN offers an inspiring, creative and unique environment to work, host meetings, conferences and events. We applied new technologies to create the best conditions possible. It boasts the fastest internet available in the CBD.

Workshop17, Waterfront, Cape Town

The Waterfront is the number one destination in Africa, with about 24 million visitors each year. Workshop17 is adding a new element to the Waterfront ecosystem of retail, residential, hospitality, entertainment, and food.

OPEN at Wits, Braamfontein, Johannesburg

OPEN at Wits is located in the new Tshimologong Precinct, initiated by the Johannesburg Centre for Software Engineering of Wits University. It is situated two blocks south of Senate House, and in the middle of Braamfontein.
